Our Story

Jaiden's Legacy: Crafting Special Memories with 18NotOut

Our eldest son Jaiden left us at the age of 18 and this firm is in memory of him. 18notout is to signify that Jaiden continues to live in our life and the memories of his lifetime are our strength. It gives us strength to know that at 18 he did not stop living but continues to live in our hearts and memories through other special children like him. With 18notout we want to help create memories for the special child and parents across the world as we believe the memories created with parents and kids are precious and if not created now would be a lost opportunity for the family to keep them strong always. We want to be known as that firm that helps in planning, managing and executing the Events/Tours/milestones for the special child and Family. Our experience in the field makes us unique and we wish to provide them a tension-free experience, where they focus only on Creating Memories.


S1E1 (Season1 Episode1)

Our first attempt is to encourage parents to join us for the Bangalore Day Trip. These parents already go through huge mental and financial stress and hence to make this possible we need to make it most affordable, safe and convenient for them to encourage them to be part of the event.
